With one item:
>>> my_list = MyList()

>>> print(my_list)
[1]

[1]
>>> print(my_list)
[1]


With negative number:
>>> my_list = MyList()
>>> my_list.append (1)
>>> my_list.append (-4)
>>> my_list.append (-2)
>>> my_list.append (7)
>>> my_list.append (3)

[-4, -2, 1, 3, 7]

[1, -4, -2, 7, 3]
